Coresignal has launched a new Company Posts Dataset that provides access to millions of structured corporate communications for market research and competitive intelligence. The dataset enables businesses to monitor competitor announcements, identify growth signals, support sales strategies, analyze corporate sentiment, and train AI systems. It includes full post content, publication dates, engagement metrics, company information, and commenter profile data, available through API endpoints and exports in JSONL and Parquet formats.
